About Me

Hi everyone! I’m Max, a Master’s student in Computer Science at Brown University. Originally from Thailand, I moved to the U.S. to earn my undergraduate degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering at Georgia Tech. There, I found my passion for electric vehicles as a member of the student Formula Electric racing team, HyTech Racing.

During the Covid-19 pandemic, I took a year away from school to work hands-on in a battery research lab with Dr. Pimpa Limthongkul, deepening my expertise in the energy sector.

Back at school, I expanded my knowledge into the transportation sector, joining the AI Institute for Advances in Optimization (AI4OPT). Under Dr. Pascal van Hentenryck’s guidance, I helped design autonomous transfer hub networks for trucks, further solidifying my drive to innovate in transportation.

I’m eager to keep advancing my skills and take on new, exciting frontiers in this field!
